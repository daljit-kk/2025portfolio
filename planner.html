St. Clair College Student Day Planner

Print Production / Brand Adherence / Layout / Contest Winner

Problem

An annual contest among all first-year graphic design students is designing a cover for the student’s annual day planner while adhering to the college’s brand guidelines and using correct print production skills.

Solution

My solution was, in relation to the back-to-school theme, a ripped paper as the focal point. It’s punchy, bold, and energetic, matching the dynamic energy of the St. Clair College campuses. My goal was to create a cover that would easily integrate with the St. Clair College family of merch. This design placed first out of 40 entries and 14,000 copies were printed and distributed on campus.
