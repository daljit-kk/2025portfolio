About

Daljit Kaur Khehra, Student RGD, is an emerging multidisciplinary designer with experience designing solutions across studio, start-up, and corporate environments. Working in these industries, Daljit has learned the art of taking highly technical information and translating it visually into engaging content different audiences can understand, pushing the envelope while working within brand guidelines, and executing projects from concept to completion within tight deadlines.

Over the past two years, Daljit’s work has been selected by the Velma Morrison Center, St. Clair College, St. Clair College Research & Innovation, and Rocket Innovation Studio (part of Rocket Companies). Moving forward, Daljit aims to influence content and overall strategic direction in the design and advertising space.
