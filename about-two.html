creativity & strategy

Daljit Kaur Khehra, Student RGD, is a multidisciplinary designer with 3 years of experience across studio, agency, start-up, and corporate environments. She has supported brands such as LEGO, Starbucks, Bausch & Lomb, Topco, Caesars Windsor, Rocket Innovation Studio (part of Rocket Mortgage), and the Patty Duke Foundation through graphic design, video editing, motion graphics, social media management, and retouching.

With a background in the sciences before pivoting to graphic design, Daljit's strengths lie in increasing brand visibility, cross-disciplinary collaboration, strategic thinking, and storytelling. Looking ahead, Daljit aims to continue shaping content and strategic direction in the design and advertising space, with the long-term goal of becoming an art director.
